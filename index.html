BetterStacks:

Description: BetterStacks is a search co-pilot tool that provides personalized recommendations using knowledge hidden in bookmarks, research notes, newsletters, and knowledge repositories across platforms.

Features:

  • Search Engine for Bookmarks: Helps users find relevant information from their saved bookmarks.
  • Personalization: Users and their teams decide search results, making it more tailored.
  • Team Collaboration: Allows teams to save, search, and organize bookmarks and resources in one place.
  • Productivity and Knowledge-Sharing: Cultivates a culture of productivity and knowledge-sharing within teams.

Product Issues and Their Importance:

  • Issue: Lack of integration with other platforms (e.g., browser extensions, mobile apps).
  • Importance: Seamless integration would enhance user experience and accessibility.

The Sticky App:

Description: The Sticky App is a tool for creating Telegram sticker packs without using the @stickers bot. It allows users to create static and animated stickers easily.

Features:

  • Telegram Sticker Maker: Enables users to create custom Telegram stickers.
  • Customization: Users can add emojis, decorations, and text to their stickers.
  • Shared Wiki for Teams: Helps teams share ideas, information, notes, files, and feedback efficiently.
  • Personal Sanctuary: Users can create a personal space on the internet, sharing content with their inner circle.
  • Data Safety: The app is not affiliated with Telegram, and users are responsible for the stickers they create.

Product Issues and Their Importance:

  • Issue: Limited platform support (currently available on Google Play).
  • Importance: Expanding to other platforms (iOS, web) would increase user reach.
